# Privacy Policy for Stroppark

**Last Updated: 16 March 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how **Stroppark** ("we", "our", or "the App") handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a transparent experience.

## 1. Information We Collect

### Precise Location Data
To provide its core functionality, Stroppark requires access to your device’s **precise location data**. 
* **How it is used:** The App uses your location to calculate the distance between your current position and nearby electric vehicle chargers or parking spaces.
* **On-Device Processing:** This location check is performed locally on your device. 

## 2. Data Storage and Sharing
* **No Server Uploads:** We do not upload, store, or monitor your location data on any external servers. 
* **No Third-Party Sharing:** Your location data is not shared with, sold to, or accessible by third-party advertisers or data brokers.
* **No Personal Identification:** We do not collect personal identifiers such as your name, email address, or phone number.

## 3. Permissions
The App will request the following permissions:
* **Access Fine Location:** Required to determine your proximity to chargers and parking.
* **Access Coarse Location:** Used as a fallback for general proximity features.

You can revoke these permissions at any time through your device settings; however, doing so will prevent the App from calculating distances to nearby facilities.

## 4. Data Retention
Because your location data is processed in real-time on your device and never stored by us, there is no data retention period. Once you close the App or the location check is complete, the data is discarded from the App's active memory.

## 5. Children’s Privacy
The App does not knowingly collect any information from children. Since no data is transmitted to our servers, we do not maintain any database of user information.

## 6. Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
